 Deviation Formula Used

σ  =  √( Σ(x − μ)² / N )
σ = Standard Deviation x = Individual login risk score μ = Mean (average) risk score N = Total number of login events Σ = Sum over all events
AI Engine: These normalized deviation scores are fed directly into the CatBoost Classifier for real-time risk prediction.
